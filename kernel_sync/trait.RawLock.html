Expand description

A low-level lock interface used by Mutex.

Implementors provide the raw operations; higher-level types (like Mutex) add RAII and safe access to protected data.

§Semantics

  • raw_lock must provide mutual exclusion and publish the critical section with acquire semantics for the caller.
  • raw_try_lock attempts to acquire without blocking.

Implementations may spin, sleep, or otherwise block, depending on context.
