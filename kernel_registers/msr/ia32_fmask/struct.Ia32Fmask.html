#[repr(transparent)]
pub struct Ia32Fmask(u64);
Expand description

IA32_FMASK — RFLAGS Mask for syscall (MSR 0xC000_0084).

On syscall, the CPU computes:

  RFLAGS := RFLAGS & ~IA32_FMASK

Bits set here are cleared in RFLAGS on entry to the kernel.
