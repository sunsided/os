Struct IdtGateAttr

#[repr(transparent)]
pub struct IdtGateAttr(u16);
Expand description

Two bytes of an IDT entry that pack:

  • low byte: IST (3 bits) + 5 reserved zero bits
  • high byte: | P | DPL(2) | S(0) | Type(4) |

On little-endian x86-64, this maps cleanly to a u16.
